What’s Included in the subscription?

+

Your subscription covers end-to-end digital design services: website & app UI/UX, brand identity, social media graphics, no-code development, and more. You'll also get unlimited requests, revisions, and ongoing support (based on your plan).

How does the design delivery process work?

+

We work in sprints. You submit your design requests, and we deliver updates every 24-48 hours. You can review, approve, or request changes instantly.

How do I provide feedback?

+

You can leave feedback directly on the designs via Figma, Loom videos, or our project management dashboard. We ensure a seamless feedback loop.

What if I'm not satisfied with the design?

+

No worries! We offer unlimited revisions. We'll keep refining the design until it matches your vision perfectly.

Which plan is right for me — Monthly or Fixed?

+

If you have ongoing design needs, the Monthly plan is best. For one-off projects with a clear scope, the Fixed price model works better.

How does the pause feature work?

+

Need a break? You can pause your subscription at any time and resume when you have more work for us. No wasted days.

Do you provide the source files?

+

Yes! Upon project completion and final payment, you will receive full ownership of all source files (Figma) and exported assets.

What design tools do you use?

+

My primary tool for UI/UX design is Figma. For vector illustrations and complex photo editing, I also utilize Adobe Illustrator and Photoshop.
